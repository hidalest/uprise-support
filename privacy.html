Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

Overview

Uprise ("the app") is built by Esteban Hidalgo ("I", "me", "my"). This Privacy Policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

Your recovery journey is deeply personal. I treat your data with the care it deserves.

Data Collected

Uprise collects only the data necessary to deliver its core functionality:

  • Name — provided via Sign in with Apple, used to personalize your experience
  • User ID — an anonymized identifier from Sign in with Apple, used to sync your data across your devices via iCloud
  • Recovery data — your streak, check-in logs, journal entries, and relapse notes, stored privately in your personal iCloud account via CloudKit

How Your Data Is Used

  • To track and display your recovery streak and progress
  • To sync your data privately across your Apple devices via iCloud
  • To surface insights and patterns from your check-in history

Your data is never sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising.

iCloud & CloudKit

Your recovery data is stored in your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit framework. I do not have access to a central server — your data lives in your iCloud, not mine. Apple's privacy policy applies to data stored in iCloud.

Sign in with Apple

Uprise uses Sign in with Apple for authentication. Apple provides your name and an anonymized user identifier. You may choose to hide your email address using Apple's private relay — the app works either way.

Data Stored Locally

Some data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted:

  • Your SOS emergency contact name and phone number
  • Notification preferences
  • App settings and avatar selection

Children's Privacy

Uprise is rated 16+ and is not intended for users under 16 years of age. I do not knowingly collect data from children under 16.

Your Rights

You can delete all your data at any time from within the app: Settings → Delete Everything. This permanently removes all local data. Your iCloud data can be deleted from your iCloud account settings.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, I will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Reach out at hidalest@icloud.com
