Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

The short version: Uprise does not collect, store, or sell your personal information. Your journal, streak, and check-in data lives on your device and in your private iCloud account. We never see it.

What data Uprise collects

Uprise stores all data — your streak, journal entries, check-ins, trigger logs, and settings — locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults and iCloud CloudKit. This data is:

  • Stored on your device
  • Synced privately through your personal iCloud account (if iCloud is enabled)
  • Never transmitted to our servers
  • Never visible to us

No account, email address, or personal identifier is required to use the app.

Optional anonymous analytics

During onboarding, you can choose to share anonymous usage data to help improve the app. This is strictly opt-in — the app works identically whether you opt in or not.

If you opt in, the following events are sent to our analytics service (Supabase):

  • App opened
  • Streak started
  • Phase advanced
  • Milestone reached
  • Check-in submitted (urge level only — no journal content)
  • Relapse logged (category only — no personal notes)
  • SOS tool opened
  • Badge earned

Each event is tagged with a randomly generated anonymous ID — a UUID created on your device and never linked to your name, Apple ID, email, or any other identifier. We cannot identify you from this data.

You can withdraw consent at any time in Settings → Privacy.

iCloud sync

If you have iCloud enabled on your device, Uprise uses Apple's CloudKit framework to sync your data across your devices. This sync occurs entirely within your personal iCloud account — Uprise has no access to it. Apple's privacy policy governs this data: apple.com/legal/privacy.

Third-party services

Supabase — used for anonymous analytics (opt-in only). No personal data is transmitted. Supabase Privacy Policy.

Formspree — used to collect email addresses submitted via the waitlist form on this website. If you submit your email, it is stored by Formspree and used only to notify you when the app launches. Formspree Privacy Policy.

Data retention

Your in-app data (streak, journal, check-ins) is retained until you delete it via Settings → Reset All Data or uninstall the app. Anonymous analytics events are retained for product improvement purposes and do not include any personal information.

Waitlist email addresses collected via the website are deleted once the app has launched and notifications have been sent.

Children's privacy

Uprise is not directed at children under 17. We do not knowingly collect any information from users under 17. If you believe a minor has submitted their data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, we will update the date above and note what changed. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
