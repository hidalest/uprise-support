Account & Data

Delete Your Account

You have full control over your data. Here's how to delete your account and all associated information from Uprise.

Option 1 — Delete from inside the app

The fastest way to erase all your data is directly from the app. This wipes your streak, check-in history, journal entries, and all locally stored information immediately.

  1. Open the Uprise app.
  2. Tap the Profile tab (bottom right).
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Everything.
  4. Confirm the deletion. All your data is erased instantly.

This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Your streak, check-ins, journal entries, and all personal data will be removed from your device and our servers.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you no longer have access to the app or prefer to request deletion directly, email us with your request. Include the email address associated with your Google account so we can locate and remove your data.

We will process your request and confirm deletion within 7 days.

Request Deletion via Email

What gets deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

  1. Streak history and start date
  2. All daily check-in entries and urge levels
  3. Journal entries and relapse logs
  4. SOS session notes and trigger entries
  5. Profile information (name, avatar selection)
  6. Your Uprise account record on our servers

Note: deleting your Uprise account does not delete your Google account. To manage your Google account, visit myaccount.google.com.
