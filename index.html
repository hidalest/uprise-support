The Phases

9 phases. One rewired brain.

Recovery follows a neurological arc. Each phase name marks a real shift in how your brain processes dopamine and reward. The orb color in the app changes as you progress.

Day 0 – 3

Ignition

Cravings are loudest right now. Anxiety, restlessness, poor sleep. This is your brain demanding what it's used to. Every hour held is real progress.

Day 3 – 7

Foundation

Gaps start appearing between urges. Sleep improves. The white-knuckle intensity of day one is easing, just a little.

Day 7 – 14

Momentum

One week in. Mood can swing. Irritability and low energy are normal. Your brain is actively rerouting its reward circuits.

Day 14 – 30

Adaptation

Many hit a "flatline": emotional numbness, low motivation, low libido. It feels wrong. It isn't. It's your dopamine system recalibrating.

Day 30 – 60

Clarity

Brain fog lifts noticeably. Focus, confidence, and emotional steadiness return. Many describe the world feeling more vivid at this stage.

Day 60 – 90

Rewiring

Triggers still exist, but they've lost their authority. Emotional depth returns. Many feel more present in conversations and relationships.

Day 90 – 180

Liberation

The 90-day reboot threshold. Self-esteem, productivity, and real-world relationships have visibly shifted. You'll feel the difference.

Day 180 – 365

Restoration

Ordinary pleasures return. Food, music, and human connection feel vivid again. Dopamine sensitivity is healing at a deep level.

Day 365+

Integration

One year. The compulsive loop is broken. A new identity, built day by day, has taken its place.

Support

Common questions.

How do I reset my streak?
Go to Settings → Danger Zone → Reset All Data. This clears your streak and all journal entries and cannot be undone.
My streak isn't counting correctly.
Make sure your device time is set automatically: Settings → General → Date & Time → Set Automatically. A manually set clock can cause miscalculations.
I got a new phone. Can I recover my data?
Yes. If iCloud was enabled on your old device, your streak and journal sync automatically when you sign into the same Apple ID on your new phone. Give it a few minutes.
Does Uprise require a subscription?
No. Core features like streak tracking, the SOS tool, and daily check-ins are free forever. An optional premium tier is coming, but the heart of the app will never be paywalled.
Is my data private?
Completely. Your data lives on your device and syncs privately through your own iCloud. We never see it, store it on our servers, or sell it to anyone.
How does the SOS tool work?
The SOS tool offers guided breathing to calm your nervous system, and a one-tap option to contact someone in your support circle. Add your contacts in Settings.
What is the Surge Protector?
After two weeks of progress, a relapse preserves a portion of your streak (70% on the first, 40% on the second) instead of resetting to zero. It's science-backed. After 14 days, your brain has begun forming new pathways worth protecting.
