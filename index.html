Support & Help

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my streak?
Go to Settings → Danger Zone → Reset All Data. This will clear your streak and all journal entries. This action cannot be undone.
My streak isn't counting correctly.
Make sure your device date and time are set to automatic: Settings → General → Date & Time → Set Automatically. Manually set clocks can cause streak miscalculations.
I got a new phone. Can I recover my streak?
Yes! If you were signed into iCloud on your old device, your data syncs automatically when you sign into the same Apple ID on your new device. Make sure iCloud is enabled and give it a few minutes to sync.
Does Uprise require a subscription?
No. Uprise is free — core features like streak tracking, the SOS tool, and daily check-ins are always free, forever. An optional subscription will unlock premium features in a future update, but the heart of the app will never be paywalled.
Is my data private?
Completely. Your data lives on your device and syncs privately through your own iCloud account. We never see it, never store it on our servers, and never sell it to anyone.
How does the SOS tool work?
The SOS tool gives you two options when an urge hits: guided breathing to calm your nervous system, or reach out to contact someone in your support circle. You can set up your support contacts in Settings.
